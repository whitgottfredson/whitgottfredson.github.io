About Me

Built for
the Sky

I'm Whit Gottfredson — a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University with an emphasis in Marketing. When I'm not studying or generating solar leads at Sunrun, I'm logging flight hours in a Cessna 172 or jumping out of perfectly good airplanes.

On February 27th, 2026, I flew solo for the first time. On the ground, I hold a USPA A-License after completing 25 jumps and all 8 levels of AFF training. Both pursuits have sharpened the same skills I bring to my professional life: calm under pressure, precise execution, and a bias toward action.
